Welcome to Wangil Investment Limited (W.I.L)

Wangil Investment Limited is a locally owned agricultural company dedicated to promoting and developing cocoa production within the Wangil Inland area of Sumgilbar Local Level Government (LLG), Sumkar District, Madang Province, Papua New Guinea.

Our mission is to empower rural communities through sustainable cocoa farming, improved agricultural practices, and increased household income opportunities. Through strategic partnerships with local farmers and stakeholders, W.I.L is committed to transforming Wangil Inland into a leading cocoa-producing region of Papua New Guinea.

Who We Are

Located at Arenduk Village, Inland Bunabun, approximately 13 kilometers from the Madang–Bogia Highway, Wangil Investment Limited serves as a catalyst for agricultural development and economic growth throughout the Wangil Inland communities.

The company is currently working with more than 1,000 cocoa farmers on a trial basis and continues to expand its support network across the region.

Our Activities

  • Distribution of cocoa seedlings to local farmers.
  • Supply of quality poly bags for nursery development.
  • Farmer training and capacity building programs.
  • Promotion of modern cocoa farming techniques.
  • Community awareness and agricultural extension services.
  • Supporting sustainable cocoa production and environmental conservation.

Vision

To establish Wangil Inland as a recognized and productive cocoa-growing region that contributes significantly to the economic development of Madang Province and Papua New Guinea.

Mission

To improve the livelihoods of rural farmers by providing quality cocoa planting materials, technical support, and training that increases cocoa productivity, quality, and long-term sustainability.

Our Goals for 2027

  • Increase cocoa nursery production throughout Wangil Inland.
  • Continue supplying poly bags and cocoa seedlings to farmers.
  • Expand participation beyond the current 1,000 farmers.
  • Deliver practical training on best cocoa management practices.
  • Improve cocoa yields and overall production quality.
  • Create sustainable income opportunities for farming households.

Supporting Local Farmers

Our farmers are the foundation of our success. Through continuous support, training, and access to quality planting materials, W.I.L seeks to help communities build stronger, more resilient agricultural enterprises that benefit future generations.
